Choco Chip Cookies

Crunchy on the outside, soft and chewy on the inside. Nothing beats a chocolate chip cookie.

Ingredients

  • 8 tbsp of Kerrygold butter
  • 1 ⅓ cups of AP flour
  • 1 cup large dark chocolate chips
  • ½ cup of white sugar
  • ½ cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp kosher salt
  • ½ tsp baking soda

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350ºF. In a pot, melt and carefully brown the butter.
  2. Let brown butter cool until warm to the touch, then add the egg, white and brown sugar until fully incorporated.
  3. Add the vanilla and salt onto the mixture. Then add the baking soda into the pool of vanilla before mixing it all well.
  4. Once the cookie mixture has a ribbon-like consistency, add the flour and chocolate chips, and then mix.
  5. Sprinkle with sea salt and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  6. Let sit and cool for 8-10 minutes, and enjoy!.

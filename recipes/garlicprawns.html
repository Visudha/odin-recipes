Garlic Shrimp

A savory summer appetizer!

Ingredients

  • 5 oz peeled garlic
  • Â½ cup olive oil, plus more for drizzling
  • 1 bunch fresh parsley and cilantro
  • 1 serrano pepper, sliced
  • 4 slices crusty bread of choice, I used sourdough
  • 2 pounds jumbo shrimp
  • Fresh lemon juice

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 F.
  2. Add garlic and oil to a small oven-safe saute pan or small baking dish.
  3. Bake for 45-60 minutes, until the garlic is soft and lightly golden. Let cool fully to room temperature.
  4. Strain the garlic from the oil and set the oil aside. Place garlic in a mortar and pestle and mash to a chunky paste. Alternately, mash with a fork, whisk, or potato masher. Set aside.
  5. Blanch the cilantro and parsely
  6. Roughly chop the herbs and then blend with the serrano and garlic and reserved garlic oil.
  7. Season the shrimp and grill it in a cast iron pan.
  8. Toss the shrimp with the garlic sauce and lemon juice
  9. Serve the shrimp over grilled bread.
